UI/UX Designer & Creative Thinker

I'm a passionate Information Technology student at Bataan Peninsula State University with a strong focus on UI/UX design and front-end development. My journey began in high school with a focus on Computer Systems Servicing, and turn into a love for creating intuitive digital experiences.

I believe that good design is not just about aesthetics but about solving problems and creating meaningful interactions. My approach combines user research, thoughtful information architecture, and visual design principles to create products that are both beautiful and functional.
